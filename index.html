Who are we ? Who are you ?


Our Vision

HENOSIS (ancient greek for oneness, unity) is a youth community based in The Hague that emboldens, empowers and connects our generation of young people.

Our Mission

We seek to provide a platform for young creatives to pursue their ideas and bring them to life.

Whether it may be a youtube series, art exhibition, sport tournament, or any other idea that you’ve had but dismissed because it was “unrealistic” or because you didn’t know where to start, together, with each of our contributions as young ambitious people and with the support of the HENOSIS partners, we can make these ideas our new realities.
